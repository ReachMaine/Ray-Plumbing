Rebate & Loan Programs

In an effort to up date aging and inefficient heating systems and other systems affecting our environment, many programs are available to our customers.

Ray Plumbing and Heating will customize a service package to suit your exclusive residential or commercial needs including:

  • MaineHousing Home Energy Loan Program
    The Home Energy Loan Program (HELP) provides very low-interest home improvement loans to low- and moderate-income homeowners to improve home energy efficiency.

  • Special Financing Plan for ENERGY STAR® and Energy Efficiency Home Improvements
    An unsecured loan program with preferential interest rates and terms designed to make fast and affordable financing from $1,000 to $20,000 available to homeowners for most types of improvements that improve the energy efficiency of your home.

  • Efficiency Maine
    In 2006 Efficiency Maine programs helped home and business owners save 74,759 MegaWatt hours of energy, worth an estimated $53.9 million in lifetime economic benefits. See if you qualify for financial aid or incentives to help you save energy.

  • USDA Rural Development
    USDA Rural Development can provide financial assistance fore plumbing and heating system replacement or repairs to those who quality. USDA has loan and grant programs that can help individuals who currently own and occupy their homes at 1% interest for qualified applicants.
