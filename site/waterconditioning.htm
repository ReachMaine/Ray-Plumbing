Water Conditioning

Ray Plumbing provides equipment and services to improve the quality of your water. Service contracts are available for maintaining your filtration equipment.

In order to select the proper filtration equipment, we need to have a current water test. Contact us for sample kits.

Once your water test has been completed, Ray Plumbing can customize a water filtering system to meet the requirements of your water.

For more information about conditioning your water, visit the National Ground Water Association or the National Rural Water Association Buyer's Guide. For additional information about private wells, visit the EPA's web site.

Call Ray Plumbing for your water Test kit. Then we will design a system to your specific conditions.
