Portable Toilet Rentals


Our monthly and weekend units are available in dark green, pewter, tan or blue. The construction units are aqua or blue.

Ray Plumbing can provide portable toilets (porta potties) for a weekend, a week or by the month. Our porta potties are often used for weddings, family reunions, and festivals for weekend or weekly rentals. Monthly rentals are typically used for camps and construction sites. Reservations are necessary to ensure your needs. Please visit our on-line rental request form to initiate a reservation.

About Our Portable Toilets:

With a Ray Plumbing portable toilet rental, you are assured of clean units in good repair and reliable service.

Our wedding units include two white toilets, 1 for men and 1 for women. Each has a hands-free washing station, vanity mirror, coat hook, anti-microbial hand soap and hand towels (no urinal).

Our monthly and weekend units are available in dark green and pewter. The construction units are aqua, tan or blue.

Features and Advantages:

  • Door mounted Mirror and Handle standard
  • Hand-Sanitizer
  • Non-splash urinal standard

  2. Determine how many people will attend. If there are multiple days, determine the peak day.
